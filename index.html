The Generations of Greek Immortals
Long ago there were gods, and before them there were titans, and even before them there were primordials.
Primordials
Commonly known as the creation deitites, these immortals were born from the nothingness of the universe. They were the first generation of immortals that ruled over the cosmos.
Titans
Childern of the primordials Gaia and Ouranus, the Titans overthrew their tyrannical father to obtain his throne. They were the second generation of deitites.
Gods
The most famous and last generation. The gods overthrew their father, the titan Kronos after he went mad with power and swallowed the first five gods. They ushered in the world that the Ancient Greeks knew and worshipped.
